⚠️ WHAT IS SATURDOOM?

SaturDoom is a weekly asset tracking tool designed to detect a potential regime shift – the moment when capital starts moving from Technology (growth) to Energy (commodities), historically a precursor to market crises.

📊 METHODOLOGY

DEFCON Panel (Threat Level):
Compares weekly performance of Energy vs Technology sectors. The DEFCON level (1-5) indicates the severity of divergence. Red = severe divergence (Tech ↓, Energy ↑), Green = calm.

Regime Shift Forecast:
Analyzes Brent (global oil), WTI (US oil), and USD index. Divergence (Brent↑, USD↑, WTI↓) gives early warning of a shift toward Energy, usually 1-3 weeks ahead.

📅 UPDATE CYCLE

Data is manually updated every Saturday based on Friday's market close (4:00 PM ET). The countdown timer shows remaining time until the next scheduled update.
